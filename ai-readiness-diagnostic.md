# AI Readiness Diagnostic

## 1. Strategy
- Is there a clear reason the organization is adopting AI?
- Are AI efforts linked to business priorities?
- Is success defined in practical terms?

## 2. Leadership and ownership
- Is there executive sponsorship?
- Is ownership clear across teams?
- Are decisions about AI coordinated or fragmented?

## 3. Use cases
- Are use cases tied to real operational problems?
- Are teams prioritizing based on value and feasibility?
- Are pilots being evaluated consistently?

## 4. Workflows
- Have teams identified workflows that need redesign?
- Is AI being added thoughtfully rather than randomly?
- Are review points and handoffs clear?

## 5. Governance
- Are there guardrails for responsible use?
- Are legal, compliance, and risk questions understood?
- Are teams clear about what requires human review?

## 6. Adoption
- Are people being trained to use AI in context?
- Are there champions or internal support structures?
- Is adoption measured beyond tool usage?

## Diagnostic question

If AI tools disappeared tomorrow, would the organization lose a real capability, or just a collection of experiments?
